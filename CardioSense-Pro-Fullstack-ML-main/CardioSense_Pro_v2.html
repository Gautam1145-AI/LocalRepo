🫀 UCI Heart Disease Dataset

Heart Disease Predictor

A clinical-grade AI tool using logistic regression trained on 303 patient records from the Cleveland Clinic Foundation to assess cardiac risk with Low / Medium / High classification.

🧠

Logistic Regression

UCI dataset–derived weights power a binary classifier mapping 13 clinical features to cardiac risk probability.

📊

~85% Accuracy

Validated on the UCI Cleveland Heart Disease dataset with standard train/test splits and cross-validation.

🎯

3-Level Risk

Low (<35%), Medium (35–65%), High (>65%) risk bands with clinical recommendations for each tier.

Feature Insights
